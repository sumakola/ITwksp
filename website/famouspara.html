Manali:
1) Manali, Himachal Pradesh is a heaven for adventure seekers.
2)Kullu Valley, Sethan, Halan, Barot and Solang Valley-Best spots in Manali for ideal short or long paragliding,skydiving sessions.
3)An hour long paragliding session will let you experience the scenic landscapes of the Himalayas, numerous meadows and water bodies.
4)For a complete adventure experience, plan camping, trekking and paragliding in Solang Valley.
5)Best time for the adventure is between February and March and May to October, except monsoons.
6)Cost: Rs. 1500 to Rs. 3500

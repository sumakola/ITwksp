VARANASI




1)Lying in the south of Uttar Pradesh, on the banks of the holy River Ganga, breathes Varanasi.
2)It is believed to be the oldest living city in the world.
3)For centuries, the mystic of this place has been attracting pilgrims from across India as well as abroad.
4)It is a heap of mismatched temples and narrow steps located on the Ganges' crescent-shaped western bank.
5)It is a city of scholars, home to one of Asia's largest universities.
6)Varanasi's legends go back some 10,000 years, to the oldest epics of Hindu literature, including the Puranas, the Vedas and Mahabharata.

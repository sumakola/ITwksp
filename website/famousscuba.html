Havelock Island-Andaman & Nicobar Islands:
Havelock Islands is one of the most popular islands belonging to A/N.
Located about 50 km away from Port Blair, the island has a wide range of exotic marine life like Humpback Parrotfish, Lionfish, soft corals, the rare dugongs and schools of colourful fishes.
There are many interesting sites upon reaching there like there is The Wall, Lighthouse, Aquarium, and Mac Point etc each with its own unique features.



The place is at its best during the months between Octobers and April, so that might just be the time to visit it otherwise the currents are simply too high and also there is a factor of monsoons
Popular Diving Sites are Barefoot Scuba, Andaman Bubbles Scuba Diving, Scuba Lov in Dive Resort, Dive India, India Scuba Explorers

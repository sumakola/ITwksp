Havelock Island-Andaman & Nicobar Islands:
1)Situated right in the middle of the Indian Ocean and Bay of Bengal, Andaman has a number of beautiful beaches and scuba diving spots.
2)Havelock Islands is one of the most popular islands.
3)Located about 50 km away from Port Blair, the island has a wide range of exotic marine life like Humpback Parrotfish, Lionfish,etc.
4)To get there, one can fetch a ferry from Port Blair which would take about 2.5 hours.
5)There are many interesting sites upon reaching there like there is The Wall, Lighthouse, Aquarium, and Mac Point etc.

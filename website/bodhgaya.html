HERITAGE

BODH GAYA




1)Bodh Gaya is located almost 96km away from Patna, Bihar and is one of the Indian heritage sites recognised by UNESCO.
2)It’s an important religious centre for the Buddhists as this was the place where Mahatma Buddha attained enlightenment.
3)The holy Bodhi Tree is the site where Siddhartha gained Enlightenment and became Gautam Buddha.
4)The famous Mahabodhi Temple was established in the reign of Ashoka the Great in about approximately 250 BCE.
5)Currently, the Mahabodhi Temple Complex at Bodh Gaya comprises the 50m tall Mahabodhi Temple, the Vajrasana.
6)The sacred Bodhi Tree and other six sacred sites of Buddha’s enlightenment, surrounded by numerous ancient Votive stupas.
7)Bodh Gaya is considered to be the holiest pilgrimage spot for the Buddhists.

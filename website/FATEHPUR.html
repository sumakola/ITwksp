HISTORICAL

FATEHPUR SIKRI




1)37 kms from Agra is built a city predominantly in Red Sandstone and is called Fatehpur Sikri.
2) This town was built by the Mughal Emperor, Akbar during 1571 and 1585.
3) This deserted city has retained many of the old structures, because of the efforts of the Archaeological department.
4) Fatehpur Sikri is one of the finest examples of Mughal architectural splendour at its height.
5) The sunset over the ruins is sight to cherish.
6) Fatehpur Sikri is the best example of the culmination of Hindu and Muslim architecture.
7) Fatehpur Sikri Mosque is said to be a copy of the mosque in Mecca and has designs, derived from the Persian & Hindu architecture.

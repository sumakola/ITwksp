HERITAGE

KHAJURAHO




1)Located in Madhya Pradesh, Khajuraho is a famous UNESCO World Heritage site in India.
2)Khajuraho is a unique heritage site which is renowned for a group of Hindu and Jain Temples situated 175 km south-east of Jhansi.
3)They are well known for their Nagara style symbolism and erotic figures and sculptures.
4) Most of these monuments were built between 950 to 1050 CE under the reign of the Chandella dynasty.
5)It consists of a total of 85 Temples spread across an area of 20 square kilometres.
6)The Kandariya temple is the most prominent of all of these temples in the Khajuraho complex.

DWARAKA




1)Located on the western tip of the Saurashtra peninsula in Gujarat, Dwarka is an ancient city of India.
2)Known as Devbhoomi Dwarka, Dwarka boasts of being the only city that is a part of both Char Dham
and also Sapta Puris mentioned in the Hindu religion.
3)Dwarka was a part of the ancient kingdom of Krishna and Nageshwar Jyotirlinga Temple.
4)For these reasons, it enjoys a remarkable religious significance and attracts thousands of pilgrims all year round.
5)The beachside and the seashores are an added tourist attraction.
6)Legend has it that the city was once engulfed by the sea and recent excavations reveal that a city did exist here.

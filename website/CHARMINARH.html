HISTORICAL

CHARMINAR




1)Charminar is a monument and a mosque, which is synonymous with the history of Hyderabad,India.
2)The majestic structure was completed in 1591 CE and is not only a landmark building of Hyderabad,India.
3) It is believed that Mohammed Quli Qutub Shahi had built the monument to commemorate the end of a deadly plague menace.
4)It is located near the bank of the river Musi.
5) Charminar is derived from two distinct words Char and Minar, which means four towers.
6)Historians believe that this structure is an example for Indo-Islamic architecture combined with few Persian elements.
7) It is believed that Hyderabad was measured in four directions from Charminar for administrative purposes.

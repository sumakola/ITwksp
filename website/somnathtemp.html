SOMNATH TEMPLE




1)The Somnath temple located in Gujarat is one of the main 12 Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva in India.
2)It is also known as “The Shrine Eternal” because it has been destroyed and plundered 16 times by invaders.
3)It has a history of being rebuilt and was most recently rebuilt in 1947.
4)As the legend goes, Somnath is believed to be built by the Moon God and the word Somnath means Protector of the Moon.
5)It is one of the most magnificent temples in India and rises to a height of about 155 feet.
6)According to tradition, the Shivalinga in Somnath is one of the 12 jyotirlingas in India.
7)The site of Somnath has been a pilgrimage site from ancient times on account of being a Triveni sangam.

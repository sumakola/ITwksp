HERITAGE

TAJ MAHAL




1)One of 7 Wonders of the World, White Marble Mughal Architecture, the Taj Mahal was built by emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaj Mahal.
2)It is located at the bank of river Yamuna in Agra.
3)It was completed in 1653 with the estimated cost of 32 million Indian rupees which would today stand up to 58 billion Indian rupees.
4)It is considered as the best example of Mughal architecture worldwide and is called the “Jewel of Muslim Art in India”.
5)This is probably the monument that is most recognised the world over for its association with the Indian heritage sites.

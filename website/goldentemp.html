SPIRITUAL

GOLDEN TEMPLE




1)Also known as Sri Harimandir Sahib, Amritsar is a central religious place of the Sikhs.
2) It is a symbol of human brotherhood and equality.
3)Everybody, irrespective of cast, creed or race can seek spiritual solace and religious fulfilment without any hindrance.
4)It also represents the distinct identity, glory and heritage of the Sikhs.
5)The temple is the focus of a complex of buildings that form the heart of Sikhism.
6)The temple occupies a small island in the centre of the tank, called the Amrita Saras (“Pool of Nectar”)—the source of the city’s name
7)It is connected to land on its west side by a marble causeway running across the water of the pool.
